Who Are We?

At Sigma Imports, we are passionate about drifting and dedicated to teaching this exhilarating motorsport to enthusiasts of all skill levels. Founded by a group of professional drift racers, our school is the premier destination for those looking to master the art of drifting.

Our mission is simple - to provide the highest quality drift training in a safe, supportive, and fun environment. We believe that drifting is more than just a motorsport; it's an art form that combines precision, control, and a bit of adrenaline-pumping excitement.

Our Story

Sigma Imports Drift School was born out of a shared passion for drifting. Our founders, a group of professional drift racers and car enthusiasts, recognized a need for a specialized drift school that could cater to the growing interest in this unique sport. With their deep knowledge and love for car culture, they set up Sigma Imports to share their skills and passion with others.

Community and culture

We are more than just a drift school; we are a community. At Sigma Imports, we foster a culture of camaraderie, respect, and continuous learning. Our students and instructors alike are part of a tight-knit family of drift enthusiasts.
